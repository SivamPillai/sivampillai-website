I had the incredible opportunity to represent Zolnoi at this year’s (2024) Fit Fest organized by Bosch (BGSW). As part of the panel discussion, I spoke about the power of generative AI beyond language models, particularly its potential in the time-series domain and real-world manufacturing applications. 💪🕝

At Zolnoi, we are focused on helping manufacturers reduce energy wastage 🌱 while increasing production capacity ⌛. Through AI-driven insights from machine and energy data, we aim to unlock hidden efficiencies on the factory floor.

During the session, I shared our vision of how AI can transform industrial operations — from detecting inefficiencies and anomalies to enabling smarter, data-driven decision making for plant teams.

It was a privilege to showcase our work and engage with a diverse audience of innovators, engineers, and industry leaders. 🚀

The conversations and connections we made reaffirmed the importance of our mission and the impact AI can have in shaping a more efficient and sustainable manufacturing sector. 🌍💡
